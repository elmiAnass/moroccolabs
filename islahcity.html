Islah.City

The islah.city project is an online platform designed to enhance civic engagement and empower citizens in Casablanca to address local issues. By reporting problems, citizens actively participate in improving their communities. This initiative aims to hold government authorities accountable for promptly and effectively resolving these issues, promoting transparency, and fostering a sense of community ownership. The project's ultimate goal is to expedite issue resolution and create a more responsive local government in Casablanca.

Expected Outcomes:

  • Empowered Citizens: islah.city will encourage active citizen participation in demanding improvements from their local administration, using simple and innovative technologies.

  • Improved City Conditions: The local government's responsiveness will lead to enhanced street conditions and prompt issue resolution, addressing concerns shared by the community.

  • Evidence-Based Public Discourse: The project will trigger a more informed public debate about potential dangers in Casablanca, better city planning, and effective solutions to the problems identified by locals.
